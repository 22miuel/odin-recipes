Descripcion

This is a pasta that is made with a pomodoro sauce with sautéed apple tomato tenderloin and julienned paprika.

ingredientes

  • pasta
  • tomato
  • garlic
  • peppers
  • tenderloin
  • Parmesan

pasos

  1. First we make the pomodoro sauce by adding tomato, garlic, paprika, chopped into large pieces and then processing it in a blender.
  2. We make a stir-fry with the loin and paprika in jlianas with finely cut garlic and chopped tomato in concassette
  3. We cook the pasta in enough water and salt (do not add oil so that the sauce sticks)
  4. In the same pan where we sautéed the meat, place the sauce to reduce said sauce. Place the sautéed meat with the sauce.
  5. To finish, we add the pasta and Parmesan cheese when plating said pasta.
