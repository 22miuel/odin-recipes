alfredo

Descripción

Alfredo pasta is a pasta with a white sauce with sautéed chicken and bacon

ingredientes

  • pasta
  • chicken
  • milk cream
  • garlic
  • onion
  • bacon
  • paremesan cheese

pasos

  1. Chop the garlic and onion into tiny cubes to sauté with the chicken and chopped bacon in a size that is pleasant to eat
  2. In the same frying pan, before it heats up, we place the heavy cream just to heat it up and make the heavy cream more liquid (be careful not to let the sauce change color).
  3. After the sauce is established, we add a generous amount of Parmesan cheese to make it more elastic.
  4. We place the already cooked pasta in the said sauce and prepared and then serve
